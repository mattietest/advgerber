Clock Design Challenge

Clock

This challenge involves you being creative and constructing a clock. The parts you must use for this challenge are:

  • 1. Q80 Quartz clock movement
  • 2. 24" by 12" 1/8" sheet of wood

You may use any other materials to construct your clock and are not limited to the two materials listed above, however they must be part of your final design.

Examples of Laser Cut clocks

Assignments

1. Create a clock using, at a minimum, the materials listed above.

2. Take a picture of the clock and post it on your portfolio.

3. Post your .cdr files of the clock on your portfolio.
