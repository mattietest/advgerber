Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Artificial intelligence is one of the newest fields in science and engineering. The name was coined in 1956 and work was started on AI in 1943. Artificial Intelligence is the development of computer systems able to perform the tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision making and translation of languages. You will engage in several activities that will engage in artificial intelligence. Machine learning is generally studying pattern recognition and computational learning theory to make predictions based upon the data by looking at algorithms.
